Data Automation: Geoduck Harvest Compliance Monitoring

Washington's Department of Natural Resources manages the state-wide wild geoduck harvest. Our compliance officers are always on-site ensuring that harvest is done within the terms of the harvest contract.

The dive officers have collected these data for decades in paper and digital logs which is a difficult dataset to analyze and to use in compliance investigations. My solution to making these data usable to our staff scientist and managers was to develop on automated field data collection process.

Using Survey123 as the field collection application and Microsoft PowerAutomate for cloud automation, we now have a continuously running process that performs basic data managment and report creation while communicating updates to field staff by email.
