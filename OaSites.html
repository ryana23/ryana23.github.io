Ocean Acidification Site Data

DNR's Acidification Nearshore Monitoring Network (ANeMoNe) collected local marine chemistry data in the shallow waters around Puget Sound. In the initial deployment, 10 sites were used to record pH, Temperature, Salinity, and Dissolved Oxygen hourly from spring of 2018 until fall of 2019.

As a more fun way to report these data to our grant funder, I created this web mapping application that shows the locations of all 10 sites, and links to their webpages and data. For more information about the ongoing ANeMoNe project please visit: https://www.dnr.wa.gov/programs-and-services/aquatics/aquatic-science/aquatic-assessment-and-monitoring-team/adaptive
