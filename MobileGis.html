Mobile GIS Storymap

A simple ESRI storymap I created to inform staff about changes to GPS use in Aquatics. I created a number of mapping applications to show what mobile is capable of, and to give our staff some insight into projects others have done.

Feel free to experiment with the eelgrass survey and submit data, watch how we are using Mobile applications to protect our state's geoduck shellfish population, and think about how much easier it is to get data collection into the hands of our staff and citizen volunteers.
